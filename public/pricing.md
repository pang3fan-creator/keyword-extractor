# Pricing — ExtractKeywords

## Free

- Price: $0/month
- Limits: 10,000 characters per submission
- Features:
  - Text keyword extraction (word frequency, density, bigrams, trigrams)
  - URL keyword extraction (fetch public web pages)
  - Results filtering (1-word, 2-word, 3-word)
  - CSV download and clipboard copy
  - No account or signup required
  - Rate-limited for fair use
