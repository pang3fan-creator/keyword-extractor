ExtractKeywords is a powerful yet simple keyword extraction tool designed for content creators, SEO professionals, and digital marketers. Whether you're optimizing blog posts, analyzing competitor content, or researching keywords for your next campaign, this tool provides instant insights without the complexity.

### How It Works

1. **Text Extraction** - Paste any text (up to 10,000 characters) and instantly get a list of keywords with frequency counts
2. **URL Analysis** - Enter a webpage URL, and the tool fetches and analyzes the content automatically
3. **Phrase Detection** - Discover 2-word (bigram) and 3-word (trigram) phrases that single-word analysis misses
