How It Works

Our keyword extraction tool analyzes your text to identify the most important terms and phrases. Using statistical analysis and natural language processing, it evaluates word frequency, term importance, and contextual relevance to surface the keywords that best represent your content.

Simply paste your text or enter a URL, click "Extract Keywords," and receive a ranked list of keywords with their frequency count and density percentage.

Why It Matters

Keyword extraction is essential for SEO optimization, content analysis, and research. Understanding which terms dominate your content helps you align with search intent, improve readability, and target the right audience. Marketers, writers, and researchers use keyword extraction to validate content strategy and discover untapped opportunities.

Use Cases

  • SEO content analysis — Ensure your content targets the right keywords and matches search intent
  • Academic research — Quickly extract key themes and concepts from papers and articles
  • Competitive analysis — Analyze competitor content to understand their keyword strategy
  • Content summarization — Get a quick overview of what a piece of content is about
  • Translation prep — Identify key terms before sending content for professional translation
